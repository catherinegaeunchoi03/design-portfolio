My Approach

01

Research-led Thinking

I start with people — observational studies, co-creative workshops, and user testing inform every design decision I make. I don't design from assumption.

02

Design with rigour

From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes, I work with clarity and intention — building components that are consistent, accessible, and handoff-ready.

03

Accountability to outcomes

Good design ships and performs. I track what I make — using analytics and behavioural data to understand impact and improve iteratively.
